Grandma's Secreat Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium size apples, peeled and sclied

Instrusctions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. in a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamoon
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamin mixture.
